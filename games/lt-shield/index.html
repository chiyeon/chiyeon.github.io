Lt. Shield is a small game made in under 48 hours for GMTK Game Jam 2020, using the Unity Engine, Aseprite, & FL Studio.



Play as Lt. Proti Shield, who mans the 'Shield Captain' position on his starship. In a role seldom called to action, he is forced into the spotlight as his starship grows increasingly out of control in the face of deadly alien encounters. Will he, his captain, and their crew make it to safety? Can he remember his academy training so long ago?
