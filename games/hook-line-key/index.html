Hook, Line, Key is a small game made in under 48 hours for Ludum Dare 48, using the Godot Engine, Aseprite, & FL Studio.



A relaxing, atmospheric fishing game about finding what was lost.


You've dropped your key into a deep, deep lake, and must progressively fish downwards in order to reach it. On the way you fish up numerous items. Can you collect all 21?
